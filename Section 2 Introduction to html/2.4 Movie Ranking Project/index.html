The Best Movies According to Sam

My top 3 Movies of ALL-TIME

The Social Network (2010)

A biographical drama about the beginnings of Facebook and the conflicts within the tech industry. It's perfect for those interested in business and innovation.

Interstellar (2014)

A sci-fi film about space exploration and family relationships, with deep themes and stunning visuals. It's ideal for those who enjoy learning.

Ex Machina (2014)

A sci-fi film that questions AI and the ethics of creating technology. This one will really make you think!
